Privacy Policy

Backdoor uses customer information to process orders, provide support, and improve the storefront experience.

Information We Collect

  • Account details such as name, email, and saved preferences.
  • Order details including products, sizes, shipping address, and fulfillment history.
  • Checkout metadata from Stripe needed to confirm payment status and order completion.

How We Use It

  • To process orders, fulfill shipments, and provide tracking updates.
  • To respond to support questions, offers, and return requests.
  • To protect the storefront against abuse, fraud, and payment risk.

Your Choices

You can contact support if you need help reviewing account information, order history, or storefront data tied to your purchases. Payment details are handled by Stripe and are not stored directly as raw card data by Backdoor.
